Accounting Reports

  • Account Totals

    Get a summary of the activity in a specific GL account for all properties. Inside, you'll see the reserve amount set on the property, the net change, and the ending balance in the selected account. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Balance Sheet

    Get a snapshot of a property's overall financial health. The Balance Sheet shows all assets, liabilities, capital, and calculates retained earnings for the current and prior years. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Balance Sheet - Comparative

    Get a snapshot of a property's overall financial health in comparison to the same period in the prior year or the prior month. The Balance Sheet shows all assets, liabilities, capital, and calculates retained earnings for the current and prior period. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Balance Sheet - Property Comparison

    Compare the balance sheets of up to 500 properties. The Balance Sheet shows all assets, liabilities, capital, and calculates retained earnings for the current and prior years. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Bank Account Activity

    Run a check register for your bank account to see how much money is actually in your bank account and spot any errors. Inside, you'll find all deposits and payments made, the date they were made and a running bank balance. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Bank Account Association

    Review which properties a bank account is associated with for accuracy. For each bank account, the cash GL account and bank account association for every property. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Cash Flow

    Track the net change in GL account along with incomings and outgoings of cash within a given time period. This report displays the net change in income, expense, and other GL accounts set to be included, along with the total cash flow for the period. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Cash Flow - 12 Month

    Track the net change in GL account along with the incomings and outgoings of cash month-over-month for a 12 month period. This report displays the net change in income, expense, and other GL accounts set to be included, along with the total cash flow for the period. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Cash Flow - Property Comparison

    Compare the net change in GL account along with incomings and outgoings of cash within a given time period for up to 950 properties. This report displays the net change in income, expense, and other GL accounts set to be included, along with the total cash flow for the period. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Cash Flow Detail

    Review in detail the net change in GL accounts in addition to the incomings and outgoings of cash within a given period. You'll find all transactions that occurred within the period that make up the cash flow report. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Chart of Accounts

    Review your chart of accounts for accuracy or gaps. You'll find the GL account number, name, account type, the cash offset account, and if it is a subaccount of any GL accounts. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Expense Distribution

    Review all bills and payment information for a given date range. This report displays bill date, amount, amount left unpaid, check number, and the property associated. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

  • General Ledger

    Audit all financial activity booked on your properties. The general ledger is the most comprehensive accounting report in AppFolio. It displays every debit, credit and running balance for every GL account in your chart of accounts. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Income Statement

    Review a property's profits and losses for a given month. Inside you'll find the total operating income, expense, and the net operating income for the month. Used primarily by presidents and accountants.

  • Income Statement - 12 Month

    Review a property's profits and losses for month-over-month. Inside you'll find the total operating income, expense, and the net operating income for the period selected. Used primarily by presidents and accountants.

  • Income Statement - Comparative

    Compare a property's profits and losses for a selected month against the same month in the prior year. Inside you'll find the total operating income, expense, and the net operating income for the month selected and the prior year. Used primarily by presidents and accountants.

  • Income Statement - Property Comparison

    Compare the profits and losses for up to 950 properties for a given period. Inside you'll find the total operating income, expense, and the net operating income for all properties selected. Used primarily by presidents and accountants.

  • Income Statement (Date Range)

    Review a property's profits and losses for a given period of time. Inside you'll find the total operating income, expense, and the net operating income for the date range selected. Used primarily by presidents and accountants.

  • Loans

    Keep a pulse on your mortgage loans payable to financial institutions in order to assess and optimize your debt financing strategy. This report displays a list of loans as of a certain date including lender, monthly payment, maturity date, ending balance, interest rate, escrow, prepayment penalty, balloon amount, etc. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Trial Balance

    Identify discrepancies in your account totals and ensure that your accounts balance for a given period of time. Inside, you'll find the GL account, the beginning balance, sum of all debits, sum of all credits, and the ending balance in the GL account. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Trial Balance by Property

    Review account balances by property for a given period of time. This report displays the property name, GL account, beginning balance, sum of all debits, sum of all credits, and the ending balance in the GL account. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Trust Account Balance

    Quickly review the amount held in trust for all properties. Inside, you'll find the operating cash balance, the security deposit cash balance, and the amount held in security deposit liability for every property. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

  • Trust Account Detail

    Audit all activity in a cash GL account across all properties. You'll find the payee/payer, transaction type, cash in/cash out, and a running balance grouped by property. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

Diagnostic Reports

  • AppFolio Stack™ Usage

    View a summary of usage for the AppFolio Stack™ partner connections your business has enabled. This report displays the properties and units connected to each integration partner.

  • Email Delivery Errors

    View all emails to residents that have delivery errors within the period data is available. Primarily used by presidents, regional managers, community managers and support staff.

  • Import Variances

    Ensure there are no issues with customer import data. This report displays list of possible issues or errors that occurred while importing information from the Spreadsheet Import/Export tool under Implementation Settings.

  • Insurance Audit

  • Late Fee Policy Comparison

    View and compare all of your late fee policies in one place. Inside, you'll see each policy that your property or current resident has had, line by line, and the date range that the policy was being used. Property-specific policies do not show an entry in the Resident column.

  • Merge Variances

    Ensure there are no issues when merging databases. This report displays a list of details and possible errors that occurred while using the Merge Databases tool under Implementation Settings.

  • Resident eCheck Fee Coverage

    View important details about resident eCheck coverage. This report displays information about coverage start and end dates and the number of billable units for covered properties. Used primarily by presidents.

  • User Roles

    Audit user permissions to decide the best user role for new users or before making user permission changes. Inside, you'll find a detailed list of all user permissions granted to each user role. Primarily used by presidents.

  • Users

    Maintain a record of all current users in your account. Inside, you'll find the user name, their email, and all property groups they have access to viewing. Used primarily by presidents and regional managers.

Leasing Reports

  • Guest Card Inquiries

    Track prospective renters for your vacancies. Inside you'll find when all inquiries were received, the current status, inquiry source, and last activity type for all guest cards. Primarily used by leasing agents, shows one row per property.

  • Guest Card Interests

    Track prospective renters for your vacancies. Inside you'll find when all interests were received, the current status, inquiry source, and last activity type for all guest cards. Primarily used by leasing agents.

  • Inactive Guest Card Interests

    Track the reasons for why prospective renters do not move forward with their guest cards.

  • Insurance Usage

    Audit Liability to Landlord insurance coverage for your units. This shows the tenant(s), the unit, and the period in which they were enrolled.

  • Lease Expiration Detail By Month

    Stay on top of expiring leases to prepare renewal offers or begin the turnover process. Run this report to view all tenants with expiring leases across properties grouped by month of lease expiration. Primarily used by property managers and leasing agents.

  • Lease Expiration Summary By Month

    Stay on top of expiring leases to prepare renewal offers or begin the turnover process. The report lists each property, the number of units, all leases expiring per month, and a total of all expiring leases for the year. Primarily used by property managers and leasing agents.

  • Lease History

    Review leases and renewals for a tenant. Inside, you'll find the lease dates, rent amounts, and renewal statuses across their entire tenancy. The rent value is calculated based on recurring charges in the Rent Income key accounts at the start of the lease term.

  • Leasing Agent Performance

    Stay informed about how effectively your leasing agents are moving through the leasing funnel. This report shows the number of showings, rental applications and converted tenants associated with a guest card. Primarily used by regional managers, property managers, and leasing agents.

  • Leasing Funnel Performance

    Stay informed about how effectively your leasing agents are moving prospects through the leasing funnel. This report captures showings, rental applications, and tenant conversions linked to each inquiry. With insights at both the agent and property levels, it helps regional and property managers, along with agents, recognize successes and uncover opportunities for growth.

  • Leasing Summary

    Stay on top of your leasing funnel by unit type. This report displays a list of unit types and leasing events such as guest cards received, rental applications received, move ins completed etc. Primarily used by regional manager, property managers, and leasing agents.

  • Occupancy Summary

    Stay on top of your portfolio's occupancy percentage. This report displays a list of unit types and occupancy statistics, such as number of occupied units, percent of units occupied, etc. Primarily used by property managers and leasing agents.

  • Owner Leasing

    View the leasing funnel for each unit in a property. Leasing events include number of inquiries received, showings, applications, and converted tenants. Primarily used by leasing agents.

  • Premium Listing Billing Detail

    View your monthly spend on Premium Listings. Inside, you'll find the name of the prospect, the date you were charged or refunded, the amount charged (or refunded), the property entered on the guest card. Primarily used by leasing agents.

  • Prospect Source Tracking

    Optimize your marketing efforts by determining which marketing sources drive the most leads. This report shows guest cards (leads), showings, rental applications (approved and denied), and converted tenants have been generated by each marketing source. Primarily used by leasing agents and property managers.

  • Renewal Summary

    Track the status of all upcoming and in-progress lease renewals offers. Inside, you'll find the tenant name, their lease dates, rent amounts, renewal status and current lease term. Primarily used by property managers.

  • Rental Applications

    Optimize your marketing efforts by determining which marketing sources drive the most converted applications. See when the application was received, a desired move in, lead source, application status, and whether the application has been screened. Primarily used by property managers and leasing agents.

  • Screening Assessments

    Stay on top of your screening process and adjust screening criteria guidelines if necessary. View screening assessments, whether or not they were overturned, and why. Primarily used by Presidents and Property Managers.

  • Screening Usage

    View overall screening activity or find and review a particular screening report. This report displays the date, unit, applicant, user who ran the screening report, your current screening package and the cost per screen grouped by property in a specified date range.

  • Showings

    Stay informed about showings. Inside, you'll see showing time, status, unit, and rental application information.

  • Unit Vacancy Detail

    Track metrics on vacancies, and decide which current and future vacancies need to be marketed. This report displays the unit status (vacant rented, unrented, notice, etc.) days vacant, if the unit is rent ready, when the unit is available, and last move out/move in dates. Used primarily by leasing agents and property managers.

Maintenance Reports

  • Project Budget Detail

    Track the key details of your project. Inside, you'll find the original budget, revisions, revised budget, billed, paid, amount remaining and notes for a particular project. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and property managers.

  • Project Directory

    Ascertain basic information about your projects. Inside, you'll find details on the budget, actuals, and more. Primarily used by presidents and property managers.

  • Purchase Order

    Maintain a high-level view of all recent purchase orders. Inside you will find created, approved and completion dates, property, vendor, status and amount. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

  • Unit Turn Detail

    Track the key details of your unit turnovers. Inside, you'll find the completion date, how many days each turn took, target days to complete a turn, labor costs, billable items, parts & supplies, PO costs, and total turn cost. Primarily used by presidents, maintenance coordinators, and property managers.

  • Vendor Directory

    Ascertain basic information about your vendors. Inside, you'll find contact information, insurance & certification expiration dates, and other general information about your vendors. Primarily used by maintenance coordinators, accountants, & accounts payable.

  • Vendor Ledger

    Track all invoices payable to a vendor. Inside, you will see all bills (paid & unpaid) and payment dates for a specific vendor. Used primarily by accountants & accounts payable.

  • Work Order

    Maintain a high-level view of all recent work orders. Inside, you'll see the status, the vendor, the property, and any billed amounts. Primarily used by maintenance coordinators.

  • Work Order Billable Detail

    Review work order billable items. Inside, you'll see all labor, inventory, and billable line item details on work orders, and any amounts they have been billed for. Useful for reviewing and reconciling all line item level billable details, especially for in-house maintenance. Primarily used by maintenance coordinators and accountants. Note that historical bill amounts may not be available (billable items and inventory will not display associated bill amounts for bills created before 03/15/2024, and labor will not display associated bill amounts for bills created before 03/01/2023).

  • Work Order Labor Summary

    Track the work performed by your in-house maintenance teams. For each maintenance technician, see the start times, end times, and total hours worked for each work order. Primarily used by Maintenance Coordinators.

Owner Reports

  • Owner Directory

    Ascertain basic information about your property owners. Inside, you'll find contact information, properties owned, and other general information about your owners. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, & property managers.

  • Owner Statement

    Provide your owners insight on the financial activity for their properties. This report shows the cash in, cash out, and any unpaid bills for an owner's properties. This report is primarily used by accountants & property managers.

  • Owner Statement (Enhanced)

    Provide your owners insight on the financial activity for their properties. This report offers further customization than the Owner Statement and shows the cash in, cash out, and any unpaid bills for an owner's properties. This report is primarily used by accountants & property managers.

  • Owner Withholdings

    Determine how much to withhold from out-of-state owners for tax purposes. Inside, enter the tax percentage and see total rent less management fees for a total amount to withhold. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    Property And Unit Reports

    • Activities Summary

      Stay up-to-date on all scheduled events and activities to ensure nothing scheduled falls through the cracks. This report lists the activity, what it's for, and who it's assigned to. Used primarily by presidents, regional managers & property managers.

    • Additional Fees

      Audit the fees currently set to be collected on each property to ensure no revenue is lost. Inside, you'll see the property, the GL account, the percent collected, and whether the fee is suppressed from the owner statement. Used primarily by presidents & accountants.

    • Amenities By Property

      View information related to amenities on specific properties, including amenity price.

    • Annual Budget - Comparative

      Compare how your property's month-to-date and year-to-date actuals vary from their budgeted figures. You'll find the MTD and YTD actuals, budget, dollar variance, and percent variance for the selected period. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Annual Budget - Forecast

      See what your property is forecasted to generate in revenue based off of the actuals for completed months and the budget for the rest of the year. Inside, you'll find the actuals for the year-to-date, the budget for all future months, and a forecasted total using both the YTD actuals and the budget. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Budget - Comparative

      Compare how your property is doing against the budgeted figures in comparison to the same period in the prior year. Inside, you'll see the actual figures, the budgeted figures, and the dollar variance for the selected period and for the same period the prior year. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Budget - Property Comparison

      Compare the actuals against the budget for up to 200 properties at once. Inside, you'll see the actual figures, the budgeted figures, and the dollar variance for the selected period. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Budget Detail

      Review the year's budgeted figures for a property or group of properties in a month-over-month report. Inside, you'll see all budgets figures month-over-month, the total budgeted income & expense, along with the net operating income budgeted. Used primarily by presidents and accountants.

    • Fixed Assets

      Maintain a high level overview of asset installation costs and practices and manage the replacement of assets over time within your properties. You'll find a list of fixed assets, status, location, and their associated costs. Used primarily by accountants and maintenance coordinators.

    • Gross Potential Rent

      Compare the maximum amount rent you could be collecting for each unit and where you may be reporting losses or gains to the market rent. Inside, you'll find the gross potential rent, the loss to market, loss to concessions, vacancy loss, delinquency loss and the net rent income. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and property managers.

    • Inventory Status

      Audit all inventory that is currently being managed in AppFolio. Inside, you'll find inventory quantity, sale price, location and any other attributes currently being tracked. Used primarily by maintenance coordinators & onsite managers.

    • Inventory Usage

      Audit where and what work inventory is being used to complete. Inside, you'll find the inventory item, the associated work order, property, and current work order status. Used primarily by maintenance coordinators & accounts payable.

    • Keys Detail

      Stay on top of the keys lent at a property. You'll see which keys are checked out, when it was checked out, and who currently has the key. Used primarily by property managers and onsite managers.

    • Property Directory

      Ascertain basic information about your properties. Inside, you'll find details on the owners, market rent, management fees, expiration dates, and more. Primarily used by presidents and property managers.

    • Property Group Directory

      Audit your property groups and portfolios to ensure employee portfolios are up-to-date. Inside, you'll find the name of the property group or portfolio and all properties assigned to it. Used primarily by presidents & regional managers.

    • Property Performance

      Get a snapshot at total revenue collected on in key income GL accounts for multiple properties at once. Inside, you'll find the total rent, application fees, late fees, and management fees collected for all properties. Add additional GL accounts for a custom view. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Rent Roll

      Get a snapshot of property's expected revenue established from existing leases in effect. Inside, you'll find market rent, actual rent charged, past due balance, deposit paid, lease dates, & occupancy percentage. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Rent Roll (Commercial)

      Get a snapshot of property's expected revenue established from existing leases in effect. Select specific GL accounts set as recurring monthly charges for an itemized view of total amounts being charged each month, 12 month total and amount per sqft. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Rent Roll (Itemized)

      Get a snapshot of property's expected revenue established from existing leases in effect. This report displays all active units' statuses, lease dates, and allows you to select specific GL accounts set as recurring monthly charges. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Rentable Items

      Audit what rentable items (such as storage, parking spots, etc.) are available and, if unavailable, who it's assigned to. Inside, you'll see the item name, who it's assigned to, charge amount, charge GL account. Used primarily by property managers and onsite managers.

    • Unit Directory

      Ascertain basic information about your properties' units. Inside, you'll find details on market rent, bed/bath count, square footage, and the default deposit charged. Primarily used by presidents and property managers.

    • Unit Inspection

      Stay on top of your routine inspections to help prevent your property from devaluing from untreated issues. Inside, you’ll find that last unit inspection date, the current tenant, and their move in/move out dates. Used primarily by property managers and onsite managers.

    • Owner 1099 Detail

      Audit all transactions that contribute to an owner's 1099 total. Inside, you'll see income totals by property owned, ownership period for the tax year. Click the income amount to view all transactions that contribute to the 1099 total. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Owner 1099 Summary

      Preview all owner 1099 totals for the tax year. Inside you will see owner information, and the 1099 total. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Vendor 1099 Detail

      Audit all transactions that contribute to a vendor's 1099 total. Inside, you'll see payment dates, amounts, and bill information for every payment made to the vendor for the tax year. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

    • Vendor 1099 Summary

      Preview all vendor 1099 totals for the tax year. Inside you will see vendor information, the 1099 payer, and the 1099 total. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

      Tenant Reports

      • Debt Collections Status

        View the status of all resident accounts placed for debt collection through an AppFolio Stack™️ collections partner. Used primarily by property managers, regional managers, and presidential users.

      • Delinquency

        Ensure timely payments of rent by locating residents with outstanding balances owed on their ledgers as of today's date. Inside, you'll find the amount receivable, the last payment amount & date. Used primarily by accountants, accounts receivable, property managers and regional managers.

      • Delinquency (As Of)

        Ensure timely payments of rent by locating residents with outstanding balances owed on their ledgers as of a certain date. Inside, you'll find the amount receivable as of a given date. Used primarily by accountants, accounts receivable, property managers and regional managers.

      • Eligible Debt Summary

        Enforce delinquencies to be paid by gaining a deeper understanding of which resident accounts are able to be submitted to collections. Inside, you'll find a list of all residents who are currently eligible to be submitted to a debt collections agency. Used primarily by property managers, regional managers, and presidential users.

      • Security Deposit Funds Detail

        Audit your liabilities on a 'per resident' level. See which residents have current balances in liability accounts on their respective tenant ledgers. Used primarily by the Accountant Role.

      • Survey Responses

        Ensure you are providing top-notch service for residents. Inside you will find survey responses from move ins, move outs, and maintenance surveys. Primarily used by maintenance coordinators, property managers, and leasing agents.

      • Tenant Directory

        Ascertain basic information about your residents. Inside, you'll find contact information, lease information, and other general information about your residents. Primarily used by leasing agents, on-site managers, property managers.

      • Tenant Ledger

        Review a tenant's full financial history since their move in. See unit info, security deposits paid, all charges, and all payments made by the occupancy. Primarily used by property managers & onsite managers.

      • Tenant Tickler

        Stay on top of important dates in the tenant life cycle. You'll find move ins, move outs, and notice dates per occupancy. Used primarily by property managers and leasing agents.

      • Tenant Transactions Summary

        Maintain a high-level view of all tenant financial activity at a property for a given month. Inside, you'll see the ledger beginning balance, ending balance, all charges/credits issued and all payments made for the month. Used primarily by accountants, accounts receivable, & property managers.

      • Tenant Unpaid Charges

        Generate an itemized statement of a tenant's outstanding charges to provide to the tenant. The charge date, amount, description, and a running balance are listed. Used primarily by property managers.

      • Tenant Unpaid Charges Summary

        Stay on top of delinquencies by generating a list of all tenants who currently have an outstanding balance. Inside, you'll find all tenants, the total amount owed, and a breakdown of total amount owed by GL Account.

      • Tenant Vehicle Info

        Audit all vehicles parked onsite at a property during walkthroughs. See the make, model, license plate, permit number, and assigned tenant for all vehicles. Used primarily by onsite managers and property managers.

      Transaction Reports

      • Aged Payables Summary

        Stay on top of bills that are overdue for payment. Inside, you'll find the payee, the total amount payable, the amount not yet due, and an aging scale. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

      • Aged Receivable Detail

        Stay on top of charges that are overdue for payment by residents. Inside, you'll find the charge date, GL account, the total amount receivable, and an aging scale. Primarily used by presidents, accountants and accounts receivable.

      • Bill Detail

        Locate paid and unpaid bills along with vendor credits. The bill detail is a flexible report for reporting on bills. Inside, you'll find the bill date, due date, the property, the payee, the total amount, and the amount paid. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

      • Charge Detail

        Review all charges dated within the selected period and see whether they've been paid or not. This report displays all paid and unpaid charges and their details in a specified date range. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, property managers, and accounts receivable.

      • Check Register

        Review all the payments made within a given period. Inside, you'll find the check number, date, payee, and payment amount. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

      • Check Register Detail

        Review all the payments made within a given period. Inside, you'll find the check number, date, payee, GL accounts and payment amount. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

      • Check Register Detail (Enhanced)

        Review all the payments made within a given period. This is the most customizable version of the check register report. Along with the basic details of a payment, this report can display the amounts for specific GL accounts included in a particular payment. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

      • Deposit Register

        Review all deposits taken to the bank for accuracy. Displays a list of deposits dated within a specified date range and the receipts that make up those deposits. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts receivable.

      • Expense Register

        Review all bills created and paid for accuracy. The Expense Register report lists all bills dated within a specified date range and provides the check details for the paid portions of the bill. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts payable.

      • Income Register

        Track all receipts entered and the charges they pay off to ensure accuracy in resident ledgers. This report displays all tenant, owner and other receipts dated in a specified date range, along with the cash and non-cash GL accounts they impacted. Primarily used by presidents, accountants, and accounts receivable.

      • Journal Entry Register

        Review all journal entries made in a given period for accuracy. Inside, you'll see the journal entry date, the property, each debit & credit, GL accounts and the JE description. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

      • Payment Plans

        Review which tenants and homeowners have charges on a payment plan to determine when cash flows are expected. This report includes the original charge amount and original charge date, along with tenant and homeowner tags, for the period range selected. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.

      • Receivables Activity

        Increase your online payment adoption by reviewing how residents are currently paying you. Inside, you'll find a list of all residents who have made a payment within the given period grouped by payment method. Primarily used by property managers and accounts receivable.

      • Resident Financial Activity

        This report displays total charges, payments and beginning & ending balances per GL accounts for all tenants in a property. Primarily used by property managers and accounts receivable.

      • Unpaid Balances by Month

        Generate a list of all tenants and homeowners who have an outstanding balance by month. This report includes property, unit, and tenant, along with total unpaid balance, for the month range selected. Primarily used by presidents and accountants.
